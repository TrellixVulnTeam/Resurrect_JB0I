Our Mission

  • Passed Friend and Family

    People can pass away at any time, causing their friends and family to feel empty without them. Having a way to be reminded of them is a great way to adjust to life without them. While pictures and videos are already there, being able to have a conversation with them would be ideal.

  • Mentally Deteriorating Elders

    In addition to passed family members and friends, significant authority figures in someoneâ€™s life become less receptive, as their mental health deteriorates. An effective alternative would be using AI to create both audio and text messages to mimic their style of conversation serving as another method for communicating for those loved elders.

  • Late Celebrities and Public Figures

    From megastar athletes to inspiring artists, many people dream to meet celebrities during their lifetime, but these opportunities are often taken away due to untimely death. Celebrities have a large social media presence, and through their social media, people gain a strong understanding of their personalities. However, having a conversation with them is still not possible, until now.

  • Counseling and Community Presence

    Lastly, we developed a counseling bot trained on data from multiple counselor forums which can be used by anyone struggling with the loss of a loved one. Our bot is trained on conversations, posts, and opinions from the most qualified counselors from different fields. Interact with the bot and ask questions to get advice and free counseling, which is very convenient considering we are in a time where getting in-person counseling is difficult.


Our Process

Find your Friends

We developed generative NLP models that requires users to input text conversations with someone. We will then process their request and return an AI adaptation that they can interact with. Users can also submit an audio clip of their friend, allowing the AI to speak with their voice. For those who have recently lost a friend or family member, this is a great coping mechanism to never lose memory of them.

Chat with Late Celebrities

We developed another AI algorithm that uses a Twitter web scraper to bring your favorite late celebrities back in an AI form. By interacting and conversing with the adaptation, you will experience a realistic interaction.

Counseling and Coping

Lastly, we developed a counseling bot trained on data from multiple counselor forums. If you are struggling with the loss of your loved one or any other mental health issues, you can use our counseling page. Interact with the bot and ask questions to get advice and free counseling, especially during a time where getting in-person counseling is difficult. Our bot is trained on conversations, posts, and opinions from the most qualified counselors from different fields.
