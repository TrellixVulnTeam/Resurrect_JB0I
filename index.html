Find your Friends

We developed an AI algorithm using PyTorch and Tensorflow that requires users to input text conversations with their loved ones. This can be someone they have lost recently, or a partner they have broken up with or an elderly who struggled to communicate. We will then process their request and return an AI adaptation that they can interact with. If you wish, you can submit an audio clip of your friend, and we can have the AI speak to you in their voice. For those who have recently lost a friend or family member, this is a great coping mechanism to never lose memory of your friend.

Chat with Late Celebrities

We developed another AI algorithm that uses a Twitter web scraper to bring your favorite late celebrities back in an AI form. By interacting and conversing with the adaptation, you will experience what an interaction with them would have been when they were still around and if you were able to meet them, fulfilling your dreams of meeting your favorite late celebrities.

Counseling and Coping

Lastly, we developed a counseling bot trained on data from multiple counselor forums. If you are struggling with the loss of your loved one or any other mental health issues, you can use our counseling page. Interact with the bot and ask questions to get advice and free counseling, especially during a time where getting in-person counseling is difficult. Our bot is trained on conversations, posts, and opinions from the most qualified counselors from different fields.
